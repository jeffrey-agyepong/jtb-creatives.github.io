ABOUT

The DMIT program at NAIT is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to succeed in the rapidly evolving digital media and IT industries. The program is structured around nine different concentrations, each of which provides students with a focused education in a particular aspect of digital media and IT.

Some of the concentrations offered by the DMIT program include Web Development, Game Design and Development, Digital Cinema, Network Systems, and Computer Software Development. Each concentration provides students with a unique set of skills and knowledge, enabling them to excel in their chosen field upon graduation.
